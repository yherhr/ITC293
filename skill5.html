Photography Skills

Using a 1987 model Penax SFX that relies on 35mm film showcases my dedication to the traditional art of photography, where each shot requires careful composition and consideration. This approach infuses my work with a nostalgic and authentic feel, capturing moments with a timeless quality that digital photography often lacks. The images on my website reflect this meticulous process, with each photograph exuding a rich, grainy texture and a depth that highlights my commitment to the craft. These images not only tell a story but also evoke emotions, inviting viewers to appreciate the nuances and subtleties of film photography.
