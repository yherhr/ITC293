Harrison's 1998 R34 Skyline

The R34 GTT, iconic in its own right, is often enhanced with the addition of 326 Yabaking Gangster Wheels, a choice revered among enthusiasts for its fusion of style and performance. Crafted with a custom aftermarket touch, these wheels boast a sleek demeanor coupled with a deep dish design, exuding an aggressive aura that perfectly complements the GTT's sporty essence. Renowned for their ability to elevate both the aesthetics and handling of vehicles, these wheels are a preferred option for those seeking to imbue their R34 GTT with a distinctively bold and dynamic presence on the road.
