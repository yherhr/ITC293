Web Development Skills

I showcase my expertise in Web development through three distinct Shopify projects: www.rokiovertime.com, www.nutraviva.com.au, and www.cosmix.com.au. Each site exemplifies my proficiency in creating visually appealing and user-friendly e-commerce platforms. For Rokiovertime, I focused on integrating custom design elements and interactive features to enhance the user experience such as custom checkout layouts and a rewards system for retuning customers. With Nutraviva, I implemented SEO strategies and optimized product listings to drive organic traffic and improve sales aswell as helped optimise for a mobile viewing experience. For Cosmix, I utilized responsive design techniques and seamless navigation to ensure an intuitive shopping experience across all devices aswell as seamless integration with their spotify account to help with user interest. These projects highlight my versatility in web development, including skills in front-end design, and e-commerce optimization.
