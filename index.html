Welcome to Krause Electric Cars!

At Krause Electric Cars we pride ourselves on our ability to:

  • Retrofit internal combustion engine vehicles into electric ones, extending their lifespan and reducing carbon emissions.
  • Utilize cutting-edge technology and expertise to seamlessly integrate electric powertrains into diverse vehicle models.
  • Offer customized solutions tailored to each client's needs, ensuring optimal performance and efficiency.
  • Provide comprehensive support and maintenance services for converted electric vehicles, ensuring longevity and reliability.
  • Champion sustainability by promoting the adoption of electric transportation, contributing to a cleaner, greener future.
  • Collaborate with automotive enthusiasts and industry partners to innovate and advance the electric vehicle conversion sector.
  • Prioritize customer satisfaction through transparent communication, quality craftsmanship, and attention to detail in every conversion project.
